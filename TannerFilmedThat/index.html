About Me

TANNERFILMEDTHAT is a professional sports photography business founded by Tanner, a passionate and experienced photographer with a keen eye for capturing the excitement and energy of live sporting events. I am able to deliver stunning and impactful images that showcase the athleticism, emotion, and drama of every game. I offer a range of photography services to clients in the sports industry, including coverage of professional and amateur sporting events, individual and team photo shoots, and promotional and marketing materials for sports organizations, sponsors, and athletes. With a commitment to quality, professionalism, and customer satisfaction.
